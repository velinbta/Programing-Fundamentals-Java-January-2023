5. Even and Odd Subtraction
Write a program that calculates the difference between the sum of the even and the sum of the odd numbers in an array.

Examples:

Input 1:
1 2 3 4 5 6

Output 1:
3

Comment 1:
2 + 4 + 6 = 12
1 + 3 + 5 = 9
12 - 9 = 3

Input 2:
3 5 7 9

Output 2:
-24

Input 3:
2 4 6 8 10

Output 3:
30
