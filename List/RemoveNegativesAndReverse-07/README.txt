7. Remove Negatives and Reverse
Read a list of integers, remove all negative numbers from it and print the remaining elements in reversed order. In case of no elements left in the list, print "empty".

Examples:

Input 1:
10 -5 7 9 -33 50

Output 1:
50 9 7 10

Input 2:
7 -2 -10 1

Output 2:
1 7

Input 3:
-1 -2 -3

Output 3:
empty
